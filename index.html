ABOUT CAMPUS CONNECT:

This project addresses two key student challenges:

  • Campus navigation
  • Accessing professor information

Our solutions to resolve this issue include the following approaches:

  • QR code-based web application for efficient navigation
  • Chatbot for streamlined access to faculty information

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use this Map?

You firstly enter the Destination of the place, then press the directions button and then enter the from location and the map guides you efficiently.

Will this be able to look for my current location?

Unfortunately no, this won't be able to detect your current location.

How frequently is the professor's timetable updated?

The professor's timetable is updated weekly by the administration. Any changes will reflect on the chatbot as soon as the updates are provided.

Do I need to be logged in?

No, you do not need to be logged in to use the map or the chatbot. They are open and accessible to all users.

Can I use the chatbot for non-professor-related inquiries?

The chatbot primarily focuses on professor-related inquiries. However, in the future, it might include features like event updates, campus news, and other student-related services.

Is there an offline version of the map?

Right now, the map is an online-only feature. However, we plan to introduce offline capabilities in future updates for ease of use without an internet connection.
